Alec's Tools
These are some tools that I've built to help with building/maintaining/fixing bikes. I hope they help!
Bike Builder
If you want to build your bike from scratch, this is your best friend. It will walk you through step by step the pieces that you need and check compatability.
Component Checker
Let's say you broke a component and don't know what to buy to replace it. This is the tool to help you pick the component that will match your current bike.
